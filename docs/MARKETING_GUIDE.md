```

- Description: 现代化的 AI 简历生成器 | Modern AI-Powered Resume Builder
- Website: 你的演示地址
- Topics: resume, ai, nextjs, typescript, react, tailwindcss, resume-builder, 
         ai-assistant, open-source, resume-generator
```

**启用功能**
- ✅ Issues
